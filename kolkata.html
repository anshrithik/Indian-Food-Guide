Top 5 Places to Enjoy Famous Foods in Kolkata

1. Peter Cat

Specialty: Chelo Kebab

Known for its famous Chelo Kebab, Peter Cat in Park Street is a must-visit restaurant in Kolkata.

2. Flurys

Specialty: English Breakfast, Pastries

A Kolkata landmark, Flurys is renowned for its English breakfast and wide variety of pastries and cakes.

3. Bhojohori Manna

Specialty: Traditional Bengali Thali

This chain of restaurants is known for serving authentic Bengali cuisine, with highlights like Shorshe Ilish and Kosha Mangsho.

4. 6 Ballygunge Place

Specialty: Bengali Sweets, Biryani

One of the best places in Kolkata for a traditional Bengali meal, with their Biryani and Mishti Doi being the top dishes.

5. Arsalan

Specialty: Kolkata Biryani

Known for its iconic Kolkata Biryani, Arsalan is a top spot for biryani lovers visiting the city.
