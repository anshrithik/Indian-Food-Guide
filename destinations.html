Famous Foods from Indian Cities

Delhi

Famous for street foods like Chole Bhature, rich, spicy Butter Chicken, and the versatile Paranthas from Paranthe Wali Gali.

Mumbai

Known for street delicacies like the iconic Vada Pav, zesty Pani Puri, and spicy Pav Bhaji, Mumbai offers a unique culinary experience.

Kolkata

Land of sweets like Rasgulla, creamy Mishti Doi, and the savory delight Kathi Rolls, Kolkata's cuisine is an experience in itself.

Chennai

A taste of South India with crunchy Dosa, fluffy Idli, and the comforting Pongal. Chennaiâ€™s food is a tribute to its rich Tamil heritage.

Bangalore

Famous for crispy Masala Dosa, paired with refreshing Filter Coffee, and the aromatic Bisi Bele Bath.

Lucknow

Rich in Nawabi heritage, Lucknow is known for its Awadhi Biryani, succulent Galouti Kebabs, and fragrant Nihari.

Jaipur

Known for its royal Rajasthani cuisine, enjoy delicacies like Dal Baati Churma, spicy Laal Maas, and sweet Ghevar.

Hyderabad

Globally famous for its fragrant Hyderabadi Biryani, the city also boasts Haleem and spicy Mirchi Ka Salan.

Amritsar

Known for rich and buttery Amritsari Kulcha, the hearty Lassi, and the flavorful Sarson Ka Saag with Makke Ki Roti.
