Top 5 Places to Enjoy Famous Foods in Bangalore

1. MTR (Mavalli Tiffin Room)

Specialty: Masala Dosa, Filter Coffee

A legendary spot for South Indian breakfast, known for its Masala Dosa and Filter Coffee.

2. Vidyarthi Bhavan

Specialty: Masala Dosa

A famous eatery serving crispy dosas for decades, Vidyarthi Bhavan is a must-visit for any food lover in Bangalore.

Specialty: Benne Masala Dosa
