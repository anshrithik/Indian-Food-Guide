Top 5 Places to Enjoy Famous Foods in Jaipur

1. Laxmi Misthan Bhandar (LMB)

Specialty: Rajasthani Thali, Ghevar

A legendary sweet shop and restaurant famous for its Rajasthani thali and traditional sweets like Ghevar.

2. Suvarna Mahal, Rambagh Palace

Specialty: Rajasthani Cuisine

An upscale restaurant offering royal Rajasthani cuisine in a grand, palace-like setting. A treat for fine dining lovers.

3. Rawat Misthan Bhandar

Specialty: Kachoris, Mirchi Vadas

4. Spice Court

Specialty: Laal Maas, Dal Baati Churma

Offering authentic Rajasthani dishes, including Laal Maas and Dal Baati Churma, Spice Court is a local favorite.

5. Chokhi Dhani

Specialty: Rajasthani Dining Experience

A themed village resort offering an immersive Rajasthani dining experience with traditional dishes, folk performances, and more.
