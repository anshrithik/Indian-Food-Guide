Featured Foods

Rosogolla - Kolkata

A soft and spongy dessert made of chhena, a traditional Bengali delicacy that melts in your mouth.

Vada Pav - Mumbai

Popular street food of Mumbai, a spicy potato fritter served in a bun with chutneys.

Chaat - Delhi

Tangy and spicy street food including various dishes like golgappa, papri chaat, and more.

Must Try Dishes

Hyderabadi Biryani

A world-famous rice dish made with fragrant basmati rice, tender meat, and rich spices. A culinary experience not to be missed.

Lucknow Kebabs

Delicious, soft kebabs, especially the famous Galouti Kebabs from Awadh. Perfectly spiced and cooked to perfection.

Fluffy idlis served with spicy sambar and coconut chutney. A staple breakfast in South India that you have to try.
