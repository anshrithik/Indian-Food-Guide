Top 5 Places to Enjoy Famous Foods in Delhi

1. Karim’s

Specialty: Butter Chicken, Kebabs

Located near Jama Masjid, Karim’s is known for its rich Mughlai cuisine. The Butter Chicken and Kebabs are must-tries.

2. Paranthe Wali Gali

Specialty: Paranthas

This famous street in Chandni Chowk is dedicated to serving a wide variety of stuffed paranthas, including potato, paneer, and even chocolate paranthas.

3. Gulati

Specialty: Chole Bhature, Dal Makhani

Located in Pandara Road, Gulati is famous for its traditional North Indian cuisine. Their Chole Bhature and Dal Makhani are legendary.

4. Sitaram Diwan Chand

Specialty: Chole Bhature

A classic spot for Chole Bhature lovers, Sitaram Diwan Chand serves a spicy and flavorful dish that attracts locals and tourists alike.

5. Indian Accent

Specialty: Modern Indian Fusion

Located in The Lodhi, Indian Accent offers a unique fusion of traditional Indian dishes with a modern twist. It’s a fine-dining experience unlike any other in Delhi.
