Top 5 Places to Enjoy Famous Foods in Lucknow

1. Tunday Kababi

Specialty: Galouti Kebabs

Famous for its Galouti Kebabs, Tunday Kababi is a legendary eatery offering authentic Awadhi cuisine.

2. Dastarkhwan

Specialty: Awadhi Biryani, Kebabs

This restaurant is known for its Awadhi Biryani, kebabs, and rich Mughlai dishes that embody Lucknow’s culinary heritage.

3. Wahid Biryani

Specialty: Biryani

A small yet popular restaurant that serves one of the best Lucknawi-style biryanis, filled with spices and flavor.

4. Royal Cafe

Specialty: Basket Chaat

A famous spot for Lucknow’s unique version of the chaat, including the famous Basket Chaat.

5. Idris Biryani

Specialty: Kolkata Biryani

Another iconic spot for biryani lovers, offering Lucknow’s unique take on this beloved dish, packed with flavors.
