Top 5 Places to Enjoy Famous Foods in Amritsar

1. Kesar Da Dhaba

Specialty: Punjabi Thali

Known for its hearty Punjabi meals, Kesar Da Dhaba is a must-visit for traditional Amritsari cuisine.

2. Bharawan Da Dhaba

Specialty: Chole Bhature

A popular spot for its delicious Chole Bhature and other North Indian delights.

3. Beera Chicken House

Specialty: Tandoori Chicken

Famous for its flavorful tandoori chicken and other Punjabi non-veg dishes.

4. Amritsari Kulcha Land

Specialty: Amritsari Kulcha

Specializing in Amritsari Kulchas, this place is a must-visit for its authentic and delicious breads.

5. Guru-Ka-Langar

Specialty: Langar Meals

Experience traditional Sikh hospitality with free, community meals at this revered institution.
