Top 5 Places to Enjoy Famous Foods in Mumbai

1. Britannia & Co.

Specialty: Berry Pulao, Salli Boti

A classic Parsi restaurant in Mumbai, known for its legendary Berry Pulao and flavorful Salli Boti.

2. Bademiya

Specialty: Kebabs

Located near Colaba, Bademiya is famous for its late-night kebabs and rolls that are a hit with locals and tourists alike.

3. Leopold Cafe

Specialty: Prawn Chilli, Beer

Iconic for its history and food, Leopold Café serves a range of dishes, but Prawn Chilli and Beer are standout favorites.

4. Trishna

Specialty: Butter Garlic Crab

Famous for its seafood, Trishna’s Butter Garlic Crab is the best in town and a must-try for seafood lovers.

5. Swati Snacks

Specialty: Pani Puri, Sev Puri

Known for its delicious street food, Swati Snacks offers Pani Puri and Sev Puri that are as authentic as they come.
